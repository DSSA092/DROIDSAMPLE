About DROID 5.0

DROID 5.0 is the upcoming three-day flagship robotics event organized by the IEEE Student Branch at the Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT), scheduled for July 13-15, 2025. Building upon the success of previous editions, this year's event aims to unite engineering enthusiasts and professionals for an immersive experience in robotics and technology.

Event Highlights:

  • Workshops: Participants can engage in hands-on sessions covering topics such as Music Signal Processing, Sound Engineering, Game Development, Dance, Coconut Shell Art, and Social Media Marketing.
  • Expert Talks: Renowned speakers from various industries will share insights on the latest trends and advancements in robotics and technology.
  • Competitions: Attendees can showcase their skills in various contests designed to challenge and inspire innovation.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded individuals, industry experts, and potential collaborators throughout the event.
